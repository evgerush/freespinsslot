  • Jurassic Park Online Slot

    Microgaming makes big bigger in the new Jurassic Park online slot. The game is based on the 1993 Spielberg hit and makes a great online slot machine. There are many winning symbols and features, including a special T-Rex Alert Mode. Keep reading to find out more.

    Game features

    The Jurassic Park online slot contains free spins, bonus rounds, and a host of exciting game symbols such as scatters and wilds as well as Running Wilds, Split Wilds, Wild Multipliers, and Wild Reels, amongst many other.
    You have 243 ways to win. These wins are awarded for adjacent symbol combinations. There is also a scatter symbol, wins from which are multiplied by the total bet stacked. These wins also add to Way wins. The scatter symbol pays in any position and the wild symbol substitutes for all other game symbols. You will find Triple stacked wilds in the Base Game.

    Special features

    There are five separate online slot bonus features during which you can win various prizes. Each round comes with its own unique symbols and features such as Free Spins, Running Wilds™, and Winning Wilds, among many more! The T-Rex Alert Mode triggers in the base game and lasts for six spins.

    Bonus Rounds

    There are five bonus features: Tyrannosaurus Rex, Velociraptor, Brachiosaurus, Triceratops, and Dilophosaurus.

    • Tyrannosaurus Rex

    This round awards up to 12 free spins with wild reels. During this round the T-Rex symbols will stack three deep on all reels. You are guaranteed a Reel Wild win when the T-Rex appears. These Free Spins cannot be retriggered.

    • Velociraptor

    The Velociraptor round comes with Split Wilds and Wild Multipliers and you can win 12 Free Spins with either. The split wilds split into two and create more winning combinations. They appear only when there is a chance of winning and when they appear they create a six of a kind win which pays 2x the five of a kind win. You will also receive a multiplier of 4x, 5x, or 6x.

    • Brachiosaurus

    This round has the Mystery Multiplier and also awards 12 free spins.

    • Triceratops

    This feature contains Running Wilds™, Random Stack Nudges, and a re-spins feature which awards 12 free spins with Running Wilds™.

    • Dilophosaurus

    This round contains Winning Wilds and awards 12 Free Spins. The Winning Wilds substitute for all symbols including the scatter and base game wild. All symbols stack three symbols deep on all reels. This feature cannot be retriggered.

    • T-Rex Alert Mode

    The T-Rex Alert Mode is triggered randomly in the Base Game and lasts for six paid spins. The feature will not trigger with the free Spins feature simultaneously and free spins cannot trigger from within the T-Rex alert Mode. While the T-Rex Alert Mode is active, the bet size will be capped at a maximum of your current bet. When the size of the bet is reduced, the amount at which the bet is capped will also reduce and you will not be able to increase your bet until this feature in over. The T-Rex Alert mode does not contains multipliers but there are triple stacked wilds on all the reels as well as individual wild symbols.
